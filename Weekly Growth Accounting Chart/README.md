# Growth Charts & Visualizations

This folder contains all visual outputs generated from the analysis.

---

## Charts Included

### 1. Weekly Growth Accounting
Visualizes:
- New users
- Retained users
- Resurrected users
- Churned users

Used to identify the **true drivers of growth**.

---

### 2. Quick Ratio Trend
Tracks growth efficiency over time:
- Quick Ratio > 1 → Healthy growth
- Quick Ratio < 1 → Unsustainable growth

---

## Why These Charts Matter
Together, these visuals answer:
- Are we growing?
- Is the growth sustainable?
- What should the product team focus on next?
