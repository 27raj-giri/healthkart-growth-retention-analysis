# Business Insights & Analysis

This folder contains the **final business-level interpretation** of the analysis.

The focus here is not on calculations, but on:
- What the data means
- Why trends are happening
- What decisions a Product Manager should consider

---

## Contents
- Insight summaries
- Growth health interpretation
- Retention vs acquisition analysis
- Product-level recommendations

---

## Objective
Translate raw metrics into **clear, decision-ready insights** that leadership or product teams can act on.
